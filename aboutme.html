My name is Matt, and I’m a photographer, filmmaker, and cinematographer based in the Charlotte, NC area.

The common theme for my work is that I am really down to work on any project I’m excited about.
For photography I am primarily interested in portraiture, but am willing to venture outside of that.
I am a big proponent of film photography, but I shoot digital as well.
For filmmaking I am primarily interested in documentary and music video, but again, I am willing to venture outside of that.
